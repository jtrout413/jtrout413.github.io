Coffee Scientist

Make Great Coffee - Repeatable

Coffee Scientist was created to make you the scientist for your coffee.

Using Coffee Scientist, you can make your favorite brews repeatable. Coffee Scientist is a brewing timer and a recipe collection all-in-one, allowing customization of every aspect.

Simply select your recipe and set the desired brew quantity. Coffee scientist will walk you through the rest, giving clear steps and guidance. From the ground beans to the brewed cup, Coffee Scientist has your back.

The Timer. Intuitive yet Powerful.

The timer is your helper. Simply select the desired brew quantity and the rest of the work will be done for you, automatically scaling each aspect of the recipe giving you a consistent taste no matter the volume.

The Recipe Collection. Organized.

This is your collection of favorite brews, sorted categorically by brewing method. Here you can create new recipes for different blends or strengths.

The Editor. Simple yet Customizable.

The editor is where the customization takes place. Every aspect of the brewing process can be finely tuned. With each stage having its unique brewing time and water amount, the possibilities are endless. Here you can make your drink your own.
